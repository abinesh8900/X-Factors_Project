XFactor was founded with the aim to be a bridge between highly skilled offshore professionals and aspiring business minds to be a part of the development of the community around us . Vision

To remain a world-class entrepreneurial, passionate, and socially responsible growth company that provides high-quality value-added services for our clients.

Make our Clients more competitive by delivering specialized outsourcing services, designed around their business and help them achieve their goals.

SOCIAL RESPONSIBILITY

We believe that the private sector has an important role in addressing social issues in the India

We recognise that our business will only thrive in communities that are equally successful.

By partnering with XFactor, you’ll be part of a company where each employee contributes part of their salary towards this noble cause, reinforced by our focus on serving our clients, developing our people and contributing to the community as responsible corporate citizens.

We take our responsibility for the future of the company and the planet seriously, which is why we hire the best people, we respect the individual, and ensure everything we do for our clients, our people and our communities is done with integrity, passion and respect.
